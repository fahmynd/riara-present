Ciela Story With Us

This boat is also perfect for those small gatherings of business colleagues. When you come to Bali for works or business, it's a good idea to let your conference delegates relax and spend some time enjoying the beautiful scenery of Bali and its neighboring islands from the sea, especially after a few days being locked away in a conference room. Everyone will really enjoy this cruise, especially the afternoon cruise where guests get to snorkel and see the marine life and then sit back relax and enjoy an enchanting sunset cruise on the way home to the Mertasari Harbor.

Our Captain and crews have over 20 years of experience cruising in Bali water and their knowledge of it is as if it was their own backyard, so you are in good hands. With that being said, the boat is also equipped with the latest navigation and safety equipment to ensure your excellent safety and great comfort.

Cultural Preservation

Ciela is a truly good looking 40-foot custom built boat. A great vessel to spend one day cruising to your favorite islands either for fishing, snorkeling or fun scuba diving. Cruising on Ciela with up to 15 of your closest families, friends or business associates would be such an exhilarating experience.
